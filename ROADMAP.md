## 📞 Getting Unstuck

When you hit a roadblock:

1. **Read error messages carefully** (they're usually helpful)
2. **Check the project README** (common issues documented)
3. **Google the exact error** (someone has hit it before)
4. **Simplify** (break down the problem)
5. **Ask for help** (communities, forums, colleagues)
